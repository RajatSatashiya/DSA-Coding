About Us
Deutsch Literary Association (DLA-VIT) aims to promote German culture & literature among students of the university. The club provides a platform for students to showcase their literary skills and guide the students interested in German literature and history to gain a deeper insight into the same. The community also works towards providing exposure about the various opportunities and potential in the nation to fellow students. Being one of the finest literary groups in the university, DLA constantly looks at doing justice to their objectives.
